Our Story

Kimidly was created by Ivan Benoît Candon, a French freelance developer with years of experience building scalable web applications and understanding the complexities of search engine optimization.

After spending countless hours manually analyzing competitors and struggling with fragmented SEO tools, Ivan identified a gap in the market for a simple, AI-powered solution that could deliver actionable insights in minutes—not hours.
